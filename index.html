Dr. Lucas Calixto

About Me

I am currently a kdb+ developer at KX. Prior I was an associate professor of mathematics at the Federal University of Minas Gerais, Brazil. I completed my Ph.D. at University of Campinas (with a one year visit at University of Ottawa in Canada) under the supervision of Adriano Moura and Alistair Savage. After that, I spent one year as a Postdoctoral Fellow at Jacobs University in Germany. My research interests are: representation theory of (quantum) Lie (super)algebras, and homological methods.
